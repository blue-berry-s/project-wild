What Project:WILD?

Project:WILD is a fictional 5v5 tactical shooter concept. It has animal based character models set in levels that evolves after round. In earlier rounds, players are forced into a chase-like game play as levels are filled with obstacles. Later gameplay involves more traditional tacitcal shooters with more open urban landscape
